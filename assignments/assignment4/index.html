Expand on Your Education

Expand on your undergraduate education through one of the more than 230 graduate and certificate programs at the University of South Carolina.

South Carolina, one of the first universities to confer the doctoral degree, established the Graduate School more than 115 years ago. Today, the university is home to more than 6,400 graduate students from 49 states and 83 different countries. With 56 nationally ranked programs, we're ready to help you excel in your field of study.
