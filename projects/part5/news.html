Potential new cure for
white-nose syndrome?
By Ava SainResearchers at the institute of Arizona think they have found a cure for white-nose syndrome. Early trials show remarkable recovery in bats with the disease and suggest that they will be successful in curing the disease responsible for killing hundreds of thousands of bats a year. Latest report on bat
deaths by wind-mills:
By John WilfongThe latest report on bat deaths due to wind mills in the greater carolina area are grim. So far over 400 bats have died flying into North Carolina wind-mills in 2023 alone. Authorities are now looking into temporary measures to deal with the problem.
