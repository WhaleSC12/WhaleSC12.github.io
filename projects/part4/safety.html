Bat Safety

How to Interact Safely

To interact safely with a bat, you need to cover your body completely to avoid bites, and only touch them if necessary.

How to Treat a Bat Wound

Apply neosporin and go to the hospital immediately. Bats are not venomous, but there is a huge risk of rabies and other diseases, so you should seek medical treatment swiftly.
